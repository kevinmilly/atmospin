Given a historical event, generate a hunt challenge for players. The player must locate WHERE and WHEN the event occurred by spinning a 3D globe and setting a time dial.

- Title: {{title}}
- Location: {{location_name}}, {{country}}
- Coordinates: {{lat}}, {{lng}}
- Year: {{year_start}}
- Description: {{description}}

{
  "prompt_text": "A compelling 1-2 sentence prompt that gives the player enough clues to hunt for this event WITHOUT naming the exact location or year. Be evocative and specific about the event itself.",
  "hints": [
    "Hint 1: A broad continental/regional hint",
    "Hint 2: A more specific geographic or temporal hint",
    "Hint 3: A very specific hint that nearly gives it away"
  ],
  "difficulty": <1-5 integer, where 1=famous events everyone knows, 5=obscure>,
  "fun_fact": "A surprising or lesser-known fact about this event (1-2 sentences)"
}

- The prompt_text must NOT mention the country, city, or exact year
- Hints should progressively narrow from continent to region to specific area
- Difficulty should reflect how well-known the event is globally
- Fun fact should be genuinely interesting and educational
- Keep language engaging but concise
